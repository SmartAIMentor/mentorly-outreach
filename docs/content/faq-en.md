# FAQ (English) — 清酒负责

> 任务 **F-2**：每周更新，目标 ≥20 条。用于 Discord pinned、官网、邮件自动回复。

## Getting started

**Q: What is this product?**  
A: An AI agent for creators — helps with content checks, brand deal fit, and repetitive publishing tasks.

**Q: Is it free?**  
A: Seed testers get early access now and a free month ($60 value) when we launch, in exchange for feedback.

**Q: How do I join as a seed user?**  
A: Reply "yes" to our outreach email or DM. We'll add you to the tester group within 48 hours.

## Account & access

**Q: Do I need a credit card to sign up?**  
A: No card required for seed access during beta.

**Q: Which platforms do you support?**  
A: TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube workflows are our focus; tell us your main platform when you reply.

**Q: I replied "yes" but haven't heard back.**  
A: Check spam; we respond within 48h. Reply again with your handle if needed.

## Credits & pricing (post product launch)

**Q: What are credits?**  
A: Credits power Chat and Create features. New users receive starter credits; you can claim daily credits and buy more.

**Q: How much does a month cost?**  
A: Seed testers receive one free month at launch; public pricing TBD.

## Product usage

**Q: What can I ask in Chat?**  
A: Content risk checks, brand deal questions, posting workflow — creator workflow topics.

**Q: Does it post for me automatically?**  
A: Not fully autopilot yet; we help with analysis, suggestions, and parts of the publish workflow.

**Q: Is my content stored?**  
A: We use your inputs to generate answers; see privacy policy on the site when live.

## Feedback

**Q: How do I report a bug?**  
A: Post in Discord #feedback or reply to your onboarding email.

**Q: What feedback do you want?**  
A: Was Chat useful? What's missing? Would you pay after the free month?
