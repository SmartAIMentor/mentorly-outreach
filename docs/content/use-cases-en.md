## Use Case 1: Pre-publish compliance check

**Scenario:** A beauty creator on TikTok drafts a sponsored post and wants to avoid shadowban triggers.

**Before:** Manually guess which phrases might hurt reach; post anyway and lose 40% impressions.

**With product:** Paste script → get flagged phrases + safer alternatives before posting.

**One-liner for marketing:** *Know what might kill your reach before you hit publish.*

---

## Use Case 2: Brand deal fit

**Scenario:** A 30k-follower lifestyle creator gets random brand DMs; unsure which deals match their audience.

**Before:** Say yes to low-fit deals or miss good ones buried in inbox.

**With product:** AI matches deals to audience profile and suggests fair rate range.

**One-liner:** *Stop guessing which brand deals actually fit your audience.*

---

## Use Case 3: Cross-platform publishing grunt work

**Scenario:** Same video goes to TikTok, IG Reels, YouTube Shorts — thumbnails, captions, compliance differ per platform.

**Before:** 2+ hours of repetitive edits every upload.

**With product:** One workflow for thumbnails, checks, and scheduling suggestions.

**One-liner:** *Ship to every platform without redoing the same work three times.*
